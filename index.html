Who I Am

I'm an undergraduate Data Science major at Northeastern Unviersity. Born and raised on Cape Cod, Massachusetts, I first learned to code while taking an intro Python class my senior year of high school. Much to the chagrin of my AP Statistics teacher I discovered that I could use my newfound knowledge to automate my work in her class, and I've been enamored with the intersection of computer science and statistics ever since.

I graduated high school in 2016 and was accepted to Northeastern under the University Scholars program: a full-tuition academic scholarship program and a vibrant community of bright minds seeking to improve the lives of others. I have completed two internships at the Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution and a co-op at Solaria Labs of Liberty Mutual Insurance.
