Guest Studies at WHOI

Between July 2016 and August 2017 while not at school I worked as a Guest Student under the Acoustic Communications Group at the Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution in Woods Hole, Massachusetts. One project I had the privelige of contributing to was the brand-new WHOI 205118a -- a low-power solution that makes use of Intel's Edison module to offer advanced control over Micro-modems and other acoustic communications equipment during deployments. As the very first user of the 205118a, my work included testing and documenting its setup, configuration, and environmental limits and providing feedback based on my results; as well as prepping boards for initial shipments to some of the Group's partners.
